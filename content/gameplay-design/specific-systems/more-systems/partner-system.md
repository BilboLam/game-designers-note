# Partner System
#### [hashtag](#parallel-presence)[Parallel Presence](/game-designers-note/gameplay-design/specific-systems/more-systems/partner-system/parallel-presence)
Players coexist in the same world without directly interacting.

#### [hashtag](#purpose-of-partner-in-game)Purpose of partner in game
- Strike a chord

- Serve as mechanic

- Enhance immersion 

Respond to player action — &#x27;Nice shot!&#x27;

- Relieve loneliness 

#### [hashtag](#separation-scenarios)Separation scenarios
- Restrict player ability 

- Advance story

- Reflect character growth

- Embody the need for each other

- Demonstrate difference in opinion

- Character death

Shatter something beautiful 

- Leave suspense if the death not confirmed
