1. [Level Design](/game-designers-note/level-design)
# arrow-progressDesign Process
When designing a new level, consider following this sequence:

#### [hashtag](#plan)[Plan](/game-designers-note/level-design/design-process/plan)
Shape the level concept before building.

#### [hashtag](#sketch)[Sketch](/game-designers-note/level-design/design-process/sketch)
Explore the space quickly before committing to details.

#### [hashtag](#blockout)[Blockout](/game-designers-note/level-design/design-process/blockout)
Build a rough white box without final art.

#### [hashtag](#review)[Review](/game-designers-note/level-design/design-process/review)
Check whether the level meets its design goals.
