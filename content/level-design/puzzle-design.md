# puzzle-piecePuzzle Design
#### [hashtag](#design-process)Design Process

1. Define the puzzle (reduce noise)

2. Add in difficulty

3. Control possibility space

#### [hashtag](#increase-difficulty)**Increase Difficulty**
- Stacking mechanics
- Use distractors to hide intent and increase difficulty
- Subvert what players assume to be true
#### [hashtag](#control-difficulty)**Control Difficulty**
- Keep questions readable — let players understand what the puzzle is asking
- Allow multiple solutions
circle-infoDont let puzzle game be a test, guide players to find the answer.

#### [hashtag](#layering)[Layering](/game-designers-note/level-design/layout/level-components/layout-patterns/functional-patterns#layering)

> Increase difficulty gradually

#### [hashtag](#hint)Hint

> Use hint carefully. They help and they spoil.

#### [hashtag](#reward)Reward

> Reward insight, reward efforts, not only reward correct answers.
