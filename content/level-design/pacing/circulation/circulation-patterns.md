# Circulation Patterns
#### [hashtag](#straight-on)Straight-On
- High Level of Authorship
- Little Player Choice Available
 

#### [hashtag](#one-way-loopback)One-Way Loopback
- Sense of Control (regional division)
- Can Seem Contrived
 

#### [hashtag](#branching-chokepoint)Branching Chokepoint
- Emphasis on Player Choice
- Create risk-reward routes
- Bigger Scope
- Choices can feel illusory

#### [hashtag](#swiss-cheese-approach)Swiss-Cheese Approach
- Emphasis on Player Choice
- Bigger Scope
- Less Design Control of early experience

#### [hashtag](#branching-fractal)Branching Fractal

#### [hashtag](#hub-and-spoke)Hub and Spoke
- Efficient: Highly Reused Hub Space
- Hub Can Become Dull/Repetitive
