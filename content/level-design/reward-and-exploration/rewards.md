# giftRewards
#### [hashtag](#reward-expectation)Reward Expectation
Mismatch between expectation and reward reduces motivation to explore. E.g. mushrooms in Elden Ring

#### [hashtag](#rewards-as-a-hint)Rewards as a hint
Small coin → big coin → hidden path

#### [hashtag](#reward-types)Reward types
Collectibles / resources / clues (notes, treasure maps)

#### [hashtag](#reward-as-endpoint)Reward as endpoint
Reward symbolizes destination; if players reach a dead end and find nothing, they may doubt if there is more ahead. BG3&#x27;s survival check chest is a fair approach to show that its just a dead end.
