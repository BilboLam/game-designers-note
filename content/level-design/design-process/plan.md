# clipboard-listPlan
At the planning stage, don&#x27;t rush into anything yet. Before you start, help yourself shape the level concept.

### [hashtag](#minimal-checklists)Minimal Checklists
- **Level Concept****.** What&#x27;s the core concept of your levels?

- **Platform.** What engine and tools will you use?

### [hashtag](#basic-checklists)Basic Checklists
- **Players&#x27; goals.** Reach the end or collect collectibles.

- **Scope.** How long is an average playthrough? How big is the level compared to player size?

- **Pillars**. What are the 2-3 most important themes/features of this project? E.g. stealth, combat, puzzle

- What abilities do players have? [What can they interact with](/game-designers-note/level-design/layout/level-components/level-elements)?

- **Asset list (basic).** What art assets and audios will you need for each level? What do you have already, and what needs to be found or made?

### [hashtag](#team-checklists)Team Checklists
For 3+ people or a series of levels.

- **Pacing.** What happens in each level? Why? How do the levels fit together?

- **Moodboard.** What are the inspirations for these levels?

- [**Worldbuilding**](/game-designers-note/narrative-design/world-building/level-design)**.** What is the lore of the level? What logic / mood guided the construction and use?
