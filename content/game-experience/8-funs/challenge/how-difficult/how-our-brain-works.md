# How our Brain Works
## [hashtag](#brain-cognitive-levels)Brain cognitive levels
#### [hashtag](#integrative-associative-intuitive)Integrative → associative → intuitive
The arc of mastering controls. At first, every input requires conscious effort. With practice, actions become fluid. Eventually they&#x27;re automatic.

#### [hashtag](#conscious-thought)Conscious thought
This is a different kind of engagement — deliberate, not reactive. E.g. in the pre-level planning phase, players think ahead: what&#x27;s the layout, what&#x27;s the threat, what&#x27;s the approach. 

#### [hashtag](#reaction-time)Reaction time
0.3s in average, 0.1s for genius

However, in games, players need more time to respond than a pure reaction test since

- Visual cues are gradual rather than sudden

- Complex environments demand three things simultaneously: 

**Attention allocation**

- **Pattern recognition**

- **Behaviour logic comprehension**
