# Match Difficulty with Player Growth
If players progressed through the game, the difficulty should grow to match the [players&#x27; growth](/game-designers-note/game-experience/the-magic-circle/flow-state/match-difficulty-with-player-growth/player-progression).

#### [hashtag](#modulate-difficulty)Modulate Difficulty

> #### [hashtag](#high-challenge-low-ability-anxiety)High challenge, low ability → anxiety

> > The Finals&#x27;s Gameplay put players in sustained pressure so easily feel anxious)

> #### [hashtag](#low-challenge-high-ability-boredom)Low challenge, high ability → boredom

#### [hashtag](#oscillate-difficulty)Oscillate Difficulty

> Give players room to breathe — so they expect next challenge more — e.g. Sekiro&#x27;s Four Easy Bosses
