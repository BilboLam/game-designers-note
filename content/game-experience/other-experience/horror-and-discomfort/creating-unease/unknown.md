- Not knowing when or where enemies come from

> E.g. Silent Hill Remake

- Out of control

> There are always multiple uneasy spots and you can&#x27;t pay attention to one without lose attention to the others — e.g. Silent Hill Remake

- Make players question their decisions

> E.g. Backrooms

- No absolute safe place
- Leaves player with no time to think
